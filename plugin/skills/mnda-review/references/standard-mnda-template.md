# Standard MNDA Template

Use this to generate a counter-proposal when a counterparty's NDA has unacceptable terms.

Collect from user before generating:
- `{company_name}` — your company's full legal name
- `{company_entity_type}` — e.g. "Delaware corporation"
- `{company_jurisdiction}` — state/country of incorporation
- `{counterparty_name}` — counterparty's full legal name
- `{counterparty_entity_type}` — their entity type
- `{counterparty_jurisdiction}` — their jurisdiction
- `{effective_date}` — e.g. "March 10, 2025"
- `{governing_law_state}` — preferred: Delaware or California
- `{agreement_term_years}` — preferred: 2
- `{notice_days}` — preferred: 30
- `{survival_years}` — preferred: 5

---

## MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (this **"Agreement"**) is entered into as of {effective_date} by and between **{company_name}**, a {company_entity_type} organised under the laws of {company_jurisdiction} (**"Company"**); and **{counterparty_name}**, a {counterparty_entity_type} organised under the laws of {counterparty_jurisdiction} (**"Counterparty"**).

### 1. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure. It excludes information that: (a) is publicly available through no fault of Receiving Party; (b) was known prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed; or (d) is legally required to be disclosed.

### 2. Mutual Confidentiality Obligations

Each Party agrees to: (a) hold the other's Confidential Information in strict confidence using at least the same degree of care as its own, but no less than reasonable care; (b) not disclose to third parties without prior written consent; (c) use solely for the Purpose; and (d) limit access to employees, contractors, and advisors with a need to know who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

### 3. Compelled Disclosure

If legally required to disclose, the Receiving Party shall: (a) promptly notify the Disclosing Party; (b) cooperate in seeking protective relief; and (c) disclose only what is legally required.

### 4. Injunctive Relief

Each Party acknowledges that breach may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages are inadequate, and the non-breaching Party shall be entitled to seek equitable relief without posting any bond.

### 5. Term and Survival

This Agreement commences on {effective_date} and continues for {agreement_term_years} years, unless earlier terminated upon {notice_days} days written notice. Confidentiality obligations survive for {survival_years} years after termination.

### 6. Return or Destruction

Upon request or termination, each Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify in writing within ten (10) business days.

### 7. No Licence

Nothing herein grants any licence under any intellectual property right. All Confidential Information remains the property of the Disclosing Party.

### 8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of {governing_law_state}, without regard to conflict of law principles.

### 9. General

(a) Entire agreement. (b) Amendments in writing only. (c) No assignment without written consent. (d) Electronic signatures valid.
